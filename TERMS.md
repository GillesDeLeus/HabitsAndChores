# Terms of Use & Community Guidelines

**Habits & Chores**
Last updated: 14 June 2026

These terms apply when you use features that involve other people — the social
features (profiles, friends) and shared **households**. Using the app purely for
your own private tasks does not require agreement to the terms below.

## Acceptable use

There is **zero tolerance for objectionable content or abusive behavior.** By
creating an account you agree that you will not:

- Use a handle, display name, avatar, or any content that is offensive,
  harassing, hateful, sexually explicit, or otherwise objectionable.
- Share objectionable content with other people through a household (for example
  in a task's title or notes). Content you put in a shared household is visible
  to the members you invite, and you are responsible for it.
- Harass, abuse, threaten, impersonate, or harm other users.
- Use the service for spam or unlawful activity.

## Moderation, reporting, and blocking

- User-entered handles and names are filtered for objectionable terms.
- You can **report** any user from their profile; reports are reviewed and
  acted upon, and reported users may have their content or account removed.
- You can **block** any user to remove them from your connections and hide them.
- We aim to act on reports of objectionable content **within 24 hours**, which
  may include removing content and ejecting the user who provided it.

## Accounts

- Accounts are authenticated with Sign in with Apple.
- You may delete your account at any time in **Settings → Account → Leave &
  delete profile**, which removes your public profile and connections.
